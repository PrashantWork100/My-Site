Pashyaart
Welcome to Pashya Art, where creativity knows no boundaries. I'm Prashant Sutar, a self-taught painter and illustrator, and this space is a testament to my journey of artistic self-discovery. Without formal training, I've forged a unique path in the realm of art, driven by passion, experimentation, and a dedication to unbridled creativity.
In the canvas of Pashya Art, my mission transcends the limitations of formal education. I strive to evoke emotions and build connections through the language of visuals. Each brushstroke narrates a story, a reflection of the power of self-taught expression.
Whether you're a seasoned art enthusiast, a collector, or someone taking their first steps into the art world, I invite you to explore the boundless creativity within Pashya Art. Connect with me on social media, and consider making a piece of this self-taught journey a part of your own story. Thank you for being a part of this unfolding chapter in artistic exploration!
